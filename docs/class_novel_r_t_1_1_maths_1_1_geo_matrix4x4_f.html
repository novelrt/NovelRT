\begin{align*} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 7 & 6 & 5\\ 2 & 1 & 4 & 3\\ 3 & 5 & 1 & 2\\ 4 & 6 & 7 & 1 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 7 & 2 & 5\\ 5 & 1 & 8 & 4\\ 8 & 3 & 2 & 2\\ 6 & 7 & 6 & 8 \end{bmatrix} &= \begin{bmatrix} 1-4 & 7-7 & 6-2 & 5-5\\ 2-5 & 1-1 & 4-8 & 3-4\\ 3-8 & 5-3 & 1-2 & 2-2\\ 4-6 & 6-7 & 7-6 & 1-8 \end{bmatrix}\\ &= \begin{bmatrix} -3 & 0 & 4 & 0\\ -3 & 0 & -4 & 1\\ -5 & 2 & -1 & 0\\ -2 & -1 & 1 & -7 \end{bmatrix} \end{align*}

Parameters
otherThe other matrix whose components get subtracted from this matrix.
Returns
This matrix where the components consist of the entrywise difference of this GeoMatrix4x4F and another GeoMatrix4x4F.
